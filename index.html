Settings

Set up your own quiz by selecting the difficulty and number of questions between 1 and 20 you would like.

How to play

  1. The default quiz consists of 9 questions.
  2. The difficulty will increase after every 3 questions.
  3. You start the quiz with 3 lives, answer incorrectly and you loose a life but can continue to answer.
  4. If your lives are depleted or you answer all questions correctly its game over!
  5. Have a look at the settings menu to configure a custom quiz!
