How to play

The quiz consists of 3 rounds, with 3 questions in each round on your chosen topic.

You start the quiz with 3 lives, answer incorrectly and you loose a life but can continue to play.

If your lives are depleted and you guess incorrectly its game over, but you ca always try again!
